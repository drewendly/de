The ability of abstraction is
fundamental to the uniqueness of
the human mind. Abstract thoughts
allow generalization and symbols
such as, language, mathematical
notation, and other ways to learn
and transmit knowledge.

As a designer I excercise the ability
to convey codefied visuals
seamlessly. The art of abstracting
creates visual language. When done
properly the viewer is able to digest
more information with less effort
and without confusion. It is visual
appeal with ergonomics.

Design serves a purpose.
Effectiveness is paramount.
